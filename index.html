ABOUT ME

Hi, I'm Krishna Sai. An aspiring Telugu-English writer passionate about storytelling, screenwriting, short films, emotional dramas, romance, friendship stories, and cinematic narratives. I write stories inspired by memories, emotions, and people we never forget.

WRITER'S NOTE

I don't write heroes. I write people. People who wait. People who regret. People who love. People who remember. Every story on this website is inspired by emotions that stay with us long after moments are gone. Some stories begin with a confession. Some stories begin with a goodbye. And some stories never begin at all. My goal is simple: To tell stories that feel real.
