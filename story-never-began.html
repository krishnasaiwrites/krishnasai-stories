A Story That Never Began

A memory that shaped a life.

Synopsis

Everything begins in the eighth grade. A new teacher enters the classroom. What starts as curiosity slowly becomes admiration. For three years he silently carries those feelings. Years later he realizes: It was never a love story. It was a memory that helped him grow.

Genre

Coming-of-Age Drama
