About The Writer

Hello, I'm Krishna Sai. An aspiring Telugu-English writer, storyteller, and screenplay writer who believes that the most powerful stories are hidden inside ordinary moments. I write stories inspired by memories, friendship, love, waiting, regret, hope, and the emotions people carry long after a moment has passed. While my academic background is in Electrical & Electronics Engineering, my passion has always been storytelling. Every person has a story. Every memory has a scene. And every emotion deserves a voice. That is why I write.

My Storytelling Philosophy

I don't write heroes. I write people. People who wait. People who remember. People who lose. People who love. My goal is not to create perfect characters. My goal is to create characters who feel real. If someone finishes reading one of my stories and says, "That feels like my life," then I have done my job.
